About ZERA

ZERA was born from a real-world problem I observed: many local entrepreneurs struggle to gather meaningful feedback on their products. As someone passionate about inclusive innovation, I created this platform to empower people with a voice in the products they use every day.

This project is part of my ALX Portfolio Project.
