About ZERA

Born from a deep desire to make user feedback more meaningful and impactful.

I created ZERA to bridge the gap between users and companies. Inspired by challenges faced by local entrepreneurs lacking direct feedback, this platform is part of my ALX Portfolio Project.

I believe people should be empowered to shape the products they use every day — and their voices deserve to be heard and rewarded.
