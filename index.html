About ZERA

Born from a deep desire to make user feedback more meaningful and impactful.

As someone passionate about inclusive innovation, I created ZERA to bridge the gap between users and companies. I was inspired by real-life challenges faced by local entrepreneurs who often lacked direct user feedback — this project is my response. It’s also part of my ALX Portfolio Project, built over several weeks of ideation, coding, testing, and iteration.

I believe that people should be empowered to shape the products they use every day — and that their voices deserve to be heard and rewarded.
